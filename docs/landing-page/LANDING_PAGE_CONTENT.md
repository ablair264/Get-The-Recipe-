## Content Strategy

### Value Propositions:

1. **Hands-Free Cooking**: Cooking Mode makes it easy to follow recipes while actively preparing food
2. **Smart Organization**: Pantry management reduces waste and shopping time
3. **Cost Savings**: Price comparison helps users find the best grocery deals
4. **International Support**: US/UK unit conversion for global recipe compatibility
5. **Seamless Integration**: Safari extension for easy recipe saving on iOS
6. **Optimized Experience**: iPad-specific layouts for enhanced usability

### Call-to-Action Updates:

- "Download Defaff Recipes Today" - More specific than generic "Get Mobile App"
- Emphasis on offline capability and cross-device sync
- "Coming soon to App Store and Google Play" with anticipation messaging
